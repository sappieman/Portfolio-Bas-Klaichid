About me

My name is Bas Klaichid, I am a student at the Media College Amsterdam, I was born in Hoorn and currently live in Oosterblokker It is pretty calm where I live so there is a lot of peace and quiet which makes it a good working environment. My choice to become a Game Developer is because I am not only curious about coding but also gameplay and level design etc.

I am currently in my third year of a 4-year Game Development program. Over these years, I have acquired knowledge in various programming languages, such as C#, Python, HTML, and JavaScript. Additionally, I have gained proficiency in using game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine 5, as well as utilizing GitHub for version control. My future career path is leaning towards working with Unreal Engine, primarily due to its impressive capabilities, enabling the creation of visually stunning games. For me working with blueprints in Unreal Engine makes programming more accessible, as it uses visual representations to clearly convey their functions. My ultimate goal is to master this skill, combining it with C++ to create outstanding game experiences.

In my free time I like to play games and workout, I also like to go out with friends and have fun. I am a relaxed person who excels in group projects, I love working with other people and the new experiences they offer.
